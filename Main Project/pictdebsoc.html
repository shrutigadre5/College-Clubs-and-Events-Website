The Genesis of DEBSOC
 DEBSOC was founded with the vision of creating a supportive environment where students could enhance their communication skills and prepare for the challenges of the real world. The founders recognized that while technical knowledge is crucial, the ability to articulate ideas clearly and persuasively is equally important. Thus, DEBSOC was established to bridge this gap and help students become well-rounded individuals.

Mission and Vision
 Mission: DEBSOC's mission is to empower students by providing them with the tools and opportunities to develop their public speaking, critical thinking, and leadership skills. The society aims to create confident, articulate, and effective communicators who can make a positive impact in their professional and personal lives.

Vision: DEBSOC envisions a community where every individual is confident in their ability to express their thoughts and ideas. The society strives to be a leading platform for nurturing oratory skills and fostering a culture of open dialogue, critical thinking, and effective leadership.
