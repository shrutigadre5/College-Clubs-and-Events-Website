Founding Vision
 PNAB was founded with a vision to enhance PICT's communication efforts by producing high-quality newsletters, brochures, and other design materials. Recognizing the importance of effective communication in promoting the college's achievements and activities, PNAB aims to uphold the highest standards of creativity, professionalism, and innovation in its publications.
Mission Statement
 The mission of PNAB is to serve as the creative and editorial backbone of PICT, producing informative and visually appealing newsletters and brochures that highlight the college's academic excellence, achievements, and cultural vibrancy. The team is dedicated to fostering a sense of pride and community among students, faculty, alumni, and stakeholders through impactful storytelling and engaging visual content.


Key Responsibilities and Activities
 PNAB is involved in a range of activities aimed at creating, designing, and disseminating informative and visually appealing publications that represent PICT's ethos and achievements.

Creation of College Newsletters
One of PNAB's primary responsibilities is the creation of college newsletters. These newsletters serve as a window into the vibrant life at PICT, featuring articles, updates, achievements, and upcoming events. PNAB meticulously curates content that resonates with the college community, ensuring that each edition informs, inspires, and engages readers.

Design and Production of Brochures
PNAB is also entrusted with designing and producing official college brochures. These brochures are critical in attracting prospective students, showcasing PICT's academic programs, faculty expertise, infrastructure, and achievements. The team employs innovative design elements and compelling visuals to create brochures that leave a lasting impression on readers and convey PICT's commitment to academic excellence and innovation.
