TEDx PICT


TEDx PICT is a non-profit organization promoting the spread of innovative ideas, best practices,since its inception in 1984. TED stands for Technology, Entertainment and Design but encompasses everything from science, business, arts to global issues. TEDxPICT is a group of proactive individuals focussed at providing a platform to bring in various stakeholders together to share the ideas, stories, and innovation. In the context of current fast developing world, sharing of ideas , and experiences is very much necessary for the overall society progress.


The Origin of TED
 TED began as a conference bringing together professionals from the fields of technology, entertainment, and design. The first conference was held in 1984, and it quickly became a biennial event. The core mission of TED is to spread ideas that can change attitudes, lives, and ultimately, the world. The tagline, "Ideas Worth Spreading," encapsulates this mission perfectly. Over the years, TED's scope has broadened to include a wide array of disciplines, reflecting the interconnectedness of knowledge and innovation.


Mission and Objectives
 The mission of TEDxPICT aligns with the broader TED mission of spreading ideas. However, it is tailored to the specific context of PICT and its surrounding community.
TEDxPICT aims to:
Inspire: Ignite curiosity and inspire innovative thinking among participants.
Connect: Build a community where diverse individuals can connect and collaborate.
Educate: Provide a platform for learning and exchanging knowledge across various disciplines.
Empower: Empower individuals to take action and implement ideas that can make a difference.
